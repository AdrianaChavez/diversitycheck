Mission

Diversity Check values company diversity and inclusivity. Before creating this website,
we saw a problem too many often face: women and minorities have no reliable source to
get the scoop on company statistics and demographic breakdowns.

Whether you're weighing two incredible job offers and want to see how you'll fit in, crave to really know which top companies are sticking to their word of
increasing employee diversity, or are simply curious to know how women are
being incorporated into tech, we hope Diversity Check is your go to
resource for company diversity analytics.

About Us


(From left) Diversity Check Founders Alessandra Hayes, Adriana Chavez, Ayelet Monas, and Haiying Man with Girls Who Code founder Reshma Saujani.

We are four very determind girls from the AppNexus New York Girls Who Code Summer Immersion
class of 2017. As women in tech ourselves, we saw a problem many face when trying to find
an inclusive workspace. We decided to devote the last two weeks of our time in SIP to creating Diversity Check to solve this problem.
