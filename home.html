The idea of this website stemmed from a field trip that we went to. During this trip, we were able to talk to other computer programmers. One of these programmers included a woman named Victoria Song. As a female minority, she explained to us that before she would walk into any interview, she would look around the room to see if anyone "looked like her." That is why we created this platform. We want to ensure that people like Victoria and other minorites don't have to look around the room to see if "anyone looks like them."
This website enables you to look at gender and ethnicity demographics of specific companies.
